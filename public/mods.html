How to Install Mods

1

Install Forge or Fabric

Download and install the appropriate mod loader for your Minecraft version. Forge is more common, but Fabric is lighter.

2

Download Mods

Download the mod files (.jar) from trusted sources like CurseForge or Modrinth. Always check compatibility.

3

Place in Mods Folder

Copy the mod files to your Minecraft mods folder. The location varies by operating system.

4

Launch and Enjoy

Start Minecraft with the mod loader profile and enjoy your enhanced mining experience!
